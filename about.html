About Me

I'm an American (and freshly made German) living in Berlin for the past 11 years, and in that time my career has brought me a range of experience in rather different professional settings - I began as an actuary, then started tutoring mathematics, later learned how to cut as a film editor, and most recently I've been assisting people relocating to Berlin - yet I never felt satisfied.

I like to learn & develop, to work both on my own & in groups, and tasks that are mentally challenging. I'd thought learning to program might fit the bill, but with the vastness of the field I never knew where to start.

Fortunately, a good friend gave me the push I needed & insisted I simply take a look at Harvard's Intro to CS course. That was it - I was hooked. Programming scratches all my preferred itches - the satisfaction of creating something from nothing, that encompasses both big picture problem solving and work of a meticulous nature. What's not to like?
